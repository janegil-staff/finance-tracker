# Qup Money

Simple. Clear. Local. Qup Money helps you track income and expenses, set savings goals, and follow your progress – all in one place.

---

## Overview

Qup Money is a personal finance tracker designed to give you control over your everyday economy. You can log income and expenses, define savings goals with amounts and deadlines, and visualize your progress to stay motivated.

> Live app: [https://qup.money](https://qup.money)

---

## Features

- **Income & Expenses:** Record transactions with categories, notes, and dates.
- **Savings Goals:** Set targets with amounts and deadlines, and track progress visually.
- **Local Context:** Built for Norwegian users with local currency and monthly overviews.
- **Dashboard:** Clear balance, goal status, and recent transactions at a glance.
- **Search & Filter:** Quickly find transactions by category, period, or text.
- **Secure Login:** Account creation with email verification.
- **Responsive Design:** Works seamlessly on mobile and desktop, with dark/light mode.
